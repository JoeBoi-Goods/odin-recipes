Description

This is my mom's recipe for BBQ sandwiches, similar to sloppy joe's.

  • 2lbs Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Green Pepper
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 1/2 c Ketchup
  • 1/2 c Brown Sugar
  • Season Salt
  1. Chop Onion and Green Pepper.
  2. Sweat veggies down for about 10 minutes.
  3. Add ground beef and break it up. Season with season salt.
  4. Once beef if fully cooked, add ketchup and brown sugar.
  5. Turn off heat. Serve on buns.
