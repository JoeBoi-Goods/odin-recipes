This corn slaw recipe is best on top of tacos. It looks nothing like the picture.

  • 1 c Corn
  • 1/2 c Sour Cream
  • 1/2 c Mayo
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Jalapeno
  • 1 Lime, juice and zest
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Chili Powder
  • Salt & Pepper
  1. Roast corn in a hot pan until lightly charred.
  2. Set aside to cool.
  3. Put all other ingredients into a bowl and mix well.
  4. Add cooled corn, and mix again.
  5. Let rest in the fridge for at least 2 hours, ideally, overnight.
