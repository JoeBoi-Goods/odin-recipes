French Bread

Home

Description

I like this french bread recipe. It is easiest with a stand mixer.

  • 1 Tbsp Yeast
  • 1 Tbsp White Sugar
  • 2 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 2 1/2 c Warm Water
  • 6-6 1/2 c All-Purpose Flour
  1. Activate the yeast.
  2. Add flour to desired consistency.
  3. Let the dough rise.
  4. Form loaves of bread.
  5. Let rise again.
  6. Prepare for baking.
  7. Bake.
